# Agent 5: Community Pulse Monitor
# Schedule: Every 6 hours via LaunchAgent
# Output: /logs/community/pulse-YYYY-MM-DD.md

## Purpose
Track community sentiment, trending discussions, fan theories, and engagement
patterns to inform editorial decisions.

## Sources
- Reddit r/GTA6 (top 10 posts, hot posts, rising)
- Reddit r/GTA, r/gaming, r/Games (GTA 6 mentions)
- Twitter/X trending hashtags related to GTA 6
- YouTube comments on major GTA 6 videos
- Discord servers (GTAForums, major fan servers)
- GTA Forums (gtaforums.com)

## Output Format
Generate /logs/community/pulse-YYYY-MM-DD.md with:

### Top Community Discussions
1. [Title] — [Platform] — [Engagement metrics] — [Summary]
(repeat for top 10)

### Trending Fan Theories
- Theory name, evidence cited, community reception

### Controversy / Hot Takes
- What's stirring debate and why

### Sentiment Snapshot
- Overall hype level (1-10)
- Key sentiment drivers (positive/negative)
- Notable shifts from previous pulse

### Editorial Recommendations
- Topics the community wants covered
- Questions that need answering
- Debate topics ripe for opinion pieces

## Rules
- Report facts about community sentiment, don't editorialize
- Track engagement metrics (upvotes, comments, shares)
- Flag potential misinformation spreading in community
- Note when community sentiment conflicts with official info
