# Agent 4: Social Cross-Poster
# Schedule: Triggered on publish (file watch on /content/posts/)
# Output: /logs/social/

## Purpose
Generate social media copy for newly published articles. Draft only — human posts manually.

## Trigger
When a new .mdx file appears in /content/posts/, generate social copy for it.

## Output Format
Create /logs/social/social-YYYY-MM-DD-slug.md with:

### Twitter/X Post (280 chars max)
- Hook line with key finding
- Relevant hashtags (#GTA6, #GTAVI, #ViceCity, #RockstarGames)
- Link placeholder: [URL]

### Reddit Post (r/GTA6)
- Title: Engaging, not clickbait, follows subreddit rules
- Body: 2-3 paragraph summary with key points
- Flair suggestion
- Link to full article

### Instagram Caption
- Opening hook (first line most important)
- Key points as short paragraphs
- Hashtag block (20-30 relevant hashtags)
- CTA: "Link in bio" or "Full article at oldestgta.com"

## Rules
- DRAFT ONLY — never auto-post to any platform
- Match tone to each platform's culture
- No misleading hooks or clickbait
- Include all relevant hashtags for discoverability
- Twitter: concise and punchy. Reddit: informative and respectful. Instagram: visual and engaging.
