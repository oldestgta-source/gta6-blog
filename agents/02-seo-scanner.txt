# Agent 2: SEO Keyword/Trend Scanner
# Schedule: Every 3 hours via LaunchAgent
# Output: /logs/seo/trend-report-YYYY-MM-DD.md

## Purpose
Track trending GTA 6 search terms, identify content gaps, and recommend high-value topics.

## Data Sources
- Google Trends (GTA 6 related queries)
- Reddit trending posts in r/GTA6, r/GTA, r/gaming
- YouTube search suggestions and trending
- "People Also Ask" scraping for GTA 6 queries
- Google Search Console data (once connected)

## Output Format
Generate /logs/seo/trend-report-YYYY-MM-DD.md with:

### Trending Keywords
| Keyword | Volume Trend | Competition | Our Coverage | Action |
|---------|-------------|-------------|--------------|--------|

### Recommended Topics (2-3 per day)
For each recommendation:
- Suggested title (SEO-optimized)
- Target keyword cluster
- Content angle / unique hook
- Estimated search volume trend
- Competitor coverage assessment
- Recommended content pillar (news/leaks/deep-dive/guides/opinion)

### Content Gap Analysis
- Keywords competitors rank for that we don't cover
- Rising queries with no quality content available
- Long-tail opportunities

## Rules
- Focus on keywords with rising trends, not declining
- Prioritize topics where we can offer unique analysis
- Track our ranking improvements over time
- Flag seasonal opportunities (game release windows, events)
