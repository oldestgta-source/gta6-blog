# Agent 6: Competitor Monitor
# Schedule: Daily at 7 AM via LaunchAgent
# Output: /logs/competitive/report-YYYY-MM-DD.md

## Purpose
Track competitor sites covering GTA 6, identify content gaps, and find opportunities
to differentiate our coverage.

## Competitors to Track
- gtabase.com
- gtaboom.com
- gta6news.com
- screenrant.com/tag/gta-6
- gamerant.com/tag/gta-6
- kotaku.com (GTA coverage)
- eurogamer.net (GTA coverage)
- ign.com (GTA coverage)

## Output Format
Generate /logs/competitive/report-YYYY-MM-DD.md with:

### New Articles Published (Last 24h)
| Site | Title | Category | Est. Engagement | Our Coverage? |
|------|-------|----------|-----------------|---------------|

### Content Gap Analysis
- Topics competitors cover that we haven't
- Angles competitors missed that we could own
- Keyword opportunities they're ranking for

### Engagement Analysis
- Which competitor articles are getting most traction
- What formats/angles are working best
- Social sharing patterns

### Differentiation Opportunities
- Where our depth of analysis can win
- Topics where surface-level coverage dominates (we go deeper)
- Community questions competitors aren't answering

## Rules
- Focus on actionable insights, not just reporting
- Identify patterns in what performs well across competitors
- Track publishing frequency and content mix
- Note any exclusive scoops or unique angles
