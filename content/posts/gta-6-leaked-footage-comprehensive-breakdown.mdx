This is a comprehensive thread documenting every detail uncovered from the GTA VI leaks. The information below is sourced from **The GTA VI Document (v1.6)**, compiled by the extended mapping and r/GTA6 Discord communities as well as GTAForums. This thread is structured as an evolving reference — not speculation, but a catalogued breakdown of what was actually found in the leaked footage and code.

*To be clear: the leaks were a terrible thing that should never have happened. We stand with the Rockstar employees who were most affected. Still, the community dissected every frame, and this is the result.*

## What Was Leaked on September 18, 2022

The original September 18 leak consisted of:

- **90 video clips** spanning from under 10 seconds to over 3 minutes, with recording dates from March 2021 to September 2022
- **2 PDF screenshots** related to camera controls and build previews for developers, from "Camera-Americas-v6-20220918_031841.pdf"
- **Python code** related to the game menu (see the Python Leak section below)
- **"GameHeaders_Tasks" C++ code** containing information about various game AI tasks, with specific references to GTA V features
- **Vehicle handling data** from GTA V's source code

## RAGE Engine Notes

The leaked footage reveals several technical details about the game engine:

- **Tweaked Euphoria** physics can be seen when enemies fall or the player jumps and ragdolls
- **Lighting and Skybox** systems from Red Dead Redemption 2 are present, including volumetric clouds
- **Heavy fog** appears in Americas 2022-04-06 15-55-26 where Lucia is in a shootout with police — far thicker than anything in GTA V, with visibility reduced to just a few meters ahead
- Some clips use **low-detail character models** labeled `low_int`
- **Character labels** in the debug are numbered based on the stage of the story
- Every **World Event** has a set of rules the player must fulfill for it to trigger, with conditions like `isPlayableCharacter` (PlayerLucia or PlayerJason) and `isWantedRule`. Green dots on the right side of each World Event show which conditions are met

## Characters

**Lucia** — One of the two protagonists. The "Bonnie" of the Bonnie & Clyde story structure reported by Jason Schreier. Possibly a hacker, based on the tools she carries (Tracker Jammer, Immobilizer Bypass, USB Drive, Auto Dialer). Her height is 5'3" (160cm). She breaks out of a cage with the line: *"Break out of the fucking cage."*

**Jason** — The other protagonist. The "Clyde" of the story. In a confirmed relationship with Lucia, as evidenced by the diner clip. His height is 6'1" (185cm).

Debug code confirms the protagonist structure: `PLAYER_LUCIA_PLAYER_STORY000_00` and `PLAYER_JASON_PLAYER_STORY000_01`.

> Disclaimer: "Bonnie and Clyde" is a key term used by Jason Schreier. This does not mean GTA VI's story will be totally inspired by it, let alone share the same ending. Schreier used it because everyone knows the reference and it made clear that Jason and Lucia are a criminal couple.

## Weapon Wheel

The weapon wheel went through multiple iterations during development, visible across several clips. The following observations are from recreations, not directly from leaked footage:

**Americas 2021-06-22 20-24-48** — Split into three sections: Weapons, Equipment, and Gear (similar to RDR2's Weapons, Items, and Horse sections). Large weapon capacity.

**Americas_1 2021-09-10 16-37-50** — Almost identical to the one above.

**Americas 2022-01-20 11-18-26** — Significant redesign. Players could hold different weapons in each hand, with an additional Quick Item Inventory at the bottom left for consumables (trauma kits, pills, cigarettes, food). Weapon capacity limited. Dual-wielding can also be confirmed in Americas_1 2022-08-29 12-44-36 through the debug menu.

**Americas 2022-06-01 17-16-15** — The latest version seen. Dual-wielding removed. Quick Inventory moved to center left. Weapon capacity still limited. Based on weapon positions, you can carry approximately: one assault weapon on your back, one handgun or semi-auto, one or two smaller melee weapons (like a knife), one or two tools/items (like a flashlight), plus an "unarmed" slot for holstering.

**Americas_1 2022-08-10 12-27-03** — Shows a health tip: *"You are injured. Your health will regenerate slowly, open your Weapon Wheel and use a recovery item to replenish your health faster."*

## New Mechanics

These are mechanics not present in GTA V. Some are from RDR2, others entirely new to the series.

**Cover and Movement:** Go prone, switch shoulders while aiming, walk while in cover, grappling during fist fights, underfire (covering face with weapons or bats, works while crouching).

**Loot System:** Jason and Lucia both carry duffle bags visible in several clips. In Americas 2021-10-29 20-21-10, Jason opens a shipping container with options for "stash gold" and "stash cash stack," placing items into his duffle bag. Cash is picked up one-by-one. There's also an animation for picking up duffle bags.

**Combat:** Downed Self-Suicide and Downed Self-Revive (option to revive yourself after heavy damage, possibly similar to RDR2 horse revival — Americas 2022-04-28 13-08-47). Ability to drop and pick up weapons (Americas_1 2021-09-10 16-37-50).

**Eagle Eye System:** Like RDR2 — possibly Jason's ability. Used to highlight items of interest such as jewelry, safes, and security cameras.

**Partner System:** Buddy Comms, Buddy Ping, command your partner during robberies. The Hank's Waffles clip shows prompts to "check in with Jason" or "hold for more options," including telling Jason to surrender or follow you. Jason and Lucia can share items — shared items tend to be weapons they can't equip, duplicates, and consumables split evenly (confirmed in Americas 2022-05-03 11-20-11 where both pick up 4x Soda and 2x Wine, sharing half of each).

**Driving:** Shooting from the car window with free aim (like the GTA San Andreas cheat code, now a core feature).

**Interaction with Objects and People:** Carry bodies (in Americas_1 2022-05-24 17-19-42, debug shows `CARRY SLOT<0>[HUMAN_CHEST]`), rob/threaten/talk with NPCs during robberies, pick up items (beer bottles and cans in Americas_1 2022-08-19 14-40-45, car parts box in Americas_1 2022-08-24 07-48-28, hats in Americas_1 2022-08-23 15-56-16).

**Weight and Muscle System (Unconfirmed):** Lucia appears thinner around the waist in some clips. Jason appears notably more muscular in the motel pool scene compared to nightclub footage, and skinnier in the motel/nightclub clips. There is also a mention of an Event called MultiGym.

## New Gameplay Systems

**Money Laundering** — An icon in the Hank's Waffles video tracks to a car wash property: a washing machine with a dollar sign in the center, the universal symbol for money laundering. Properties can apparently be purchased for laundering purposes.

**Purchasable Businesses** — Likely linked with the laundering system.

**Robberies** — Expanded robbery system with multiple event types.

**Fences** — Car Fence (mentioned under collectibles) and Art Fence are both in the events list. Iris is connected to the Art Fence (via "Watching Iris" and "Iris Introduce Art Fence" events). Though it is unclear if they will be called fences in-game.

**Hacking** — Lucia carries Tracker Jammer, Immobilizer Bypass, USB Drive, Auto Dialer. Could indicate Tom Henderson's old leak about the female hacker protagonist.

**Command Your Partner** — During the Hank's Waffles robbery, prompts allow checking in with Jason, holding for more options, telling Jason to surrender, or telling him to follow you.

**Vehicle Discovery/Compendium** — In Americas 2021-10-29 20-21-10, Jason rides a Maibatsu Sanchez and the UI shows a new livery has been found.

**Sharing Items** — Jason and Lucia can share items between them. In Americas 2021-10-29 20-21-10, Jason keeps certain items from containers while sharing others. Shared items are generally weapons they can't equip or already have, food and ammo, while tools (Immobilizer Bypass, Auto Dialer for Lucia) are kept by the individual.

**Unlocking Doors/Gates** — In Americas 2022-02-10 20-25-15, debug text shows `DoorPanel: Locked` on a garage, and `Door: Unlocked` on a gate.

**Witness System and Police Recognition** — During the Hank's Waffles robbery, text under the wanted level stars reads "Full Description" — witnesses have a full description of you. When Lucia enters a police vehicle, it changes from "No Vehicle Description" to "Full Vehicle Description." A tip reads: *"Any vehicle you are seen entering will be known by the Law."* Jason prevents customers with yellow icons from calling cops or running. When a female NPC's icon starts flickering and turns red, she leaves the diner and runs.

**Pragmatic Cool/Chaotic & Romantic Cool/Chaotic** — Mentioned in the events list alongside "Radio Song/Riding Pillion." Pillion is a term for the rear passenger seat.

**Store Banning** — Players can get banned from certain stores (Supermarket, Quickshop, Uncle Jack's Liquor) as a consequence of prior actions, similar to RDR2.

**Tourist to Local Progression** — Events track: Checking out → Experienced Tourist → Full Tourist → Nearly a Local.

## New Features

### Surrender to Police

In Americas 2021-07-12 12-19-51, Lucia can tell Jason to surrender to the police during a robbery.

### Improved AI System

**Enemy AI** — Enemies shoot when you turn your back to them (Americas 2022-02-07 14-18-40). Police adjust their stance depending on cover height, lower position when reloading, keep shooting while moving left and right (Americas 2022-07-22 15-33-32). Police raise guns over higher objects like cars in the Hank's Waffles clip.

**NPC Behavior** — NPCs travel and converse in groups (like RDR2). In one clip, three men in tourist attire walk past Lucia while talking. A man enters the nightclub as Lucia exits. NPCs stand and drink near bars (Americas_1 2022-04-12 10-28-55). Some NPCs sit on the ground with animations like `Sit_Ground_Smoke`. Attacked NPCs run with phones in hand (Americas_1 2022-04-13 09-08-46). When cops arrive at Hank's Waffles, an NPC says *"Jesus, finally."*

**Partner AI** — Lucia follows Dre (not just Jason) in the nightclub (Americas 2022-08-03 10-05-49), suggesting the AI knows where to go without tracking only the player character. A locomotion team test clip exists for this mechanic (Americas_1 2022-09-12 16-42-30). Lucia automatically turns around when shot, and both protagonists have specific damage animations (Americas 2022-07-22 15-33-32).

### Police System

**Time Until Cops Dispatch** — A dispatch timer appears when committing crimes. At Hank's Waffles, police wait for the player to come out rather than rushing in.

**Cop Traps** — Events at many locations suggesting police ambushes: Alley, Apartment Complex, Bingo, Body Shop, Bulk Storage, Burnout Scooters, Cafe Carraway, Cash n Carry, Closing Store, Food Trucks, Kington Hotel, Massage Parlors. They seem to coincide with Easy Score events.

**CCTV System** — A CCTV Detection Meter exists, suggesting security cameras play a role in wanted level and stealth.

**Ability to Restrain NPCs** — Lucia uses what appears to be zipties in the Hank's Waffles robbery.

### Vehicle Systems

**Loot Vehicles** — A prompt reads "Examine - Stranger SUV" in the Hank's Waffles clip.

**Advanced Car Hijacking** — Immobilizer Bypass for luxury cars, Slim Jim for older cars. Events exist for: Steal Car - Full, Steal Car - In Progress, Steal Car Fail, Carjacking - Cat, Carjacking - Advanced AI.

**More Detailed Car Interiors** — Americas 2022-09-13 17-17-43.

**Vehicle Controls** — D-PAD left for vehicle controls, right for quick options (from Hank's Waffles video).

**Improved Vehicle Damage** — The car crash in Americas 2022-06-27 14-52-04 shows the front fender splitting apart and hood bending realistically, with spark particles at impact with bridge edges. Car handling seems heavier than GTA V.

**First-Person GPS** — Car interiors now have working GPS/waypoints on the dashboard while in first-person view. The map shown is a placeholder of a GTA V location.

**Passenger Seat Option** — Lucia enters only the passenger seat in one clip, implying this is an option.

### Character Systems

**Dirty Clothes** — Suggested by Americas_1 2022-09-12 14-42-12 showing Jason with dirty clothes.

**Hair and Facial Hair** — Various versions of Jason appear across clips with different hair lengths and beard states, suggesting RDR2-style hair growth or a deeper barber system. In Americas_1 2022-09-13 16-24-02, Lucia appears to be wearing makeup with a different hair color, and her hair is straight compared to her normal curly ponytail style.

**Eating and Drinking** — Wine, Soda, and Fruit appear in the inventory at a gas station. You can eat or drink from inventory at any time, like RDR2 and GTA Online snacks.

**Smoking** — A cigarette icon is visible in the weapon wheel in Americas 2022-06-01 17-16-15.

**Gumball Machines** — You can buy gumballs (Americas_1 2022-07-20 17-59-49).

**Playing Without Minimap** — On-screen waypoints and map icons suggest this is possible. The minimap appears when Jason gets on a bike (Americas 2021-10-29 20-21-10).

**Greet/Antagonize** — Confirmed by a "Sun Lotion (Greet)" event, meaning this system from RDR2 returns.

Dead leaves on the ground in Rockridge (Americas_1 2022-09-12 14-42-12). The dithering fade effect loads objects into the world gradually, making transitions smoother.

Jack of Hearts nightclub has TVs in DVD Player mode. Bars and public areas have TV broadcasting news and events.

**Physics** — Cops struggle to stand after being shot, avoiding pressure on injured body parts — one tries to stand while protecting the injured area (Americas 2022-04-06 15-55-26). Bullets scratch through vehicles creating sparks (Americas 2022-06-14 19-28-07). Water particles float when shot, and lily pads react to waves (Americas 2022-04-12 18-36-00). Sheriffs in passenger seats look out side windows to check for cross traffic. Birds stand on power lines.

**Lighting** — The nighttime lighting creates realistic orange tints on roads with bright spots where lights are and accurate reflections. The fog in the shootout clip gives the illusion of strong hurricane-season winds.

**Store Systems** — Some stores are not open 24/7 (a "Sorry\! WE'RE CLOSED" sign appears on a Quickshop door in Americas_1 2022-07-20 17-59-49, a texture that also appears in GTA Vice City: The Definitive Edition). Collision boxes exist for chip bags outside stores, suggesting manual food purchasing like RDR2. Doors feel physically attached and locked/unlocked, unlike GTA V's floating door feel. Stores have more variety due to the focus on robberies.

## State of the Game

Analysis of video metadata reveals three executable types (credit to TezFunz2):

- **game_win64_bankrelease.exe** — A debug build aiming to be "feature complete" (full UI functionality, core gameplay features all present and playable)
- **game_win64_beta.exe** — Adds extra functionality, aiming for "content complete" (all game content present but may be visually unrefined with bugs)
- **game_win64_debug.exe** — Standard debug build

The progression from bankrelease to beta in the September 2022 clips suggests the game was moving from feature-complete to content-complete status. From content-complete, teams typically enter a "polishing" phase of variable duration. For comparison, Final Fantasy XVI announced content-complete status in June 2022 and launched June 22, 2023.

This may be a hood near Downtown with African-American street gangs. The Getaway Driver events suggest players can choose their getaway driver based on skill levels, similar to GTA V heist crew selection. The Safe House event suggests you can have a safe house in the area.
